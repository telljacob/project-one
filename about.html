Who I am

My name is Agnes Strand, I am 26 years old and I have worked with photography for the past 6 years and got my photography degree from Jönköping University prior to that.

I've had a passion for photography since I was very young. I love capturing people, expressions and forms. Creating art and memories that last forever.

I also thrive on helping new aspiring models experience what it's all about and together build their portfolio and improving.

Outside of work I try to spend my time outdoors, hiking, running and traveling. Hopefully I will one day be able to travel through my work aswell.
